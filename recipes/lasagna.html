Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and melted cheese. It’s hearty, comforting, and perfect for family dinners.

The combination of savory flavors and the satisfying texture of the layers make it a favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce (24 oz)
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic in olive oil until the beef is browned and the onion is soft.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the pan and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and chopped basil together.
  6. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish, starting with a layer of sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes to brown the cheese.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
